Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 24, 2026

Simple Summary: Taskra stores all your tasks locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal data. Your tasks never leave your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Taskra ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our task management application.

2. Information We Collect

We don't collect any personal information. Taskra is designed with privacy in mind:

  • No Account Required: You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use Taskra.
  • Local Storage Only: All your tasks, notes, and app settings are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage.
  • No Server Communication: Your task data never leaves your device or gets uploaded to our servers (because we don't have any!).
  • No Analytics: We don't use analytics tools or tracking software.
  • No Cookies: We don't use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

Since we don't collect any information about you, we can't use it! All app functionality happens locally on your device.

4. In-App Purchases

Taskra offers a one-time premium upgrade through Google Play Billing. When you make a purchase:

  • Google Handles Payment: All payment processing is handled by Google Play. We never see or store your payment information.
  • Purchase Verification: Google Play provides a purchase token that's stored locally on your device to verify your premium status.
  • No Personal Data: We don't receive any personal information from Google about your purchase beyond the verification token.

5. Permissions

Taskra requests the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To remind you about tasks with due dates (optional, you control this).
  • Internet Access: Only used for downloading the app and verifying in-app purchases with Google Play. Your task data is never transmitted.

6. Data Security

Your task data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. The security of this data depends on your device's security:

  • Keep your device locked with a PIN/password/biometric authentication
  • Keep your operating system updated
  • Only install apps from trusted sources

7. Data Backup and Loss

Important: Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • If you uninstall Taskra, your tasks will be deleted
  • If you switch devices, your tasks won't transfer automatically
  • If you clear app data/cache, your tasks will be lost
  • We cannot recover your tasks if they're deleted

Recommendation: Regularly screenshot important task lists or back up your device.

8. Third-Party Services

Taskra uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Play Billing: For processing in-app purchases. Google's privacy policy applies: https://policies.google.com/privacy

9. Children's Privacy

Taskra is suitable for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any personal information, there's no special consideration needed for users under 13 years old.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We recommend checking this page periodically.

11. Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data:

  • You have complete control over your data (it's on your device)
  • You can delete all data by clearing app data or uninstalling
  • There's no data to request, export, or delete from our servers

12. International Users

Taskra works offline and stores data locally. No matter where you are in the world, your data stays on your device and is subject to your local device security.
